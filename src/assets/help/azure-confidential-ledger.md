### Azure Confidential Ledger

A managed service providing tamper-proof ledger storage built on CCF.

### Setup Steps

1. **Backup your ledger** - Create a ledger backup
2. **Generate SAS token** - Log into your storage account
3. **Provide SAS URL** - Enter token in the form below
